Sponsorship

Benefits

  • Reach over 13 million people who visit the mall each year
  • Deliver your message to more than 300,000 people attending rink activities
  • Target audience youth to seniors
  • Provides a venue for publicity and promotion of your business

Sponsorship Opportunities

Dasher Boards
Put your company in front of our skaters and spectators. Display your logo and message on the 30" by 8' boards surrounding the ice. We are limited to 10 dasher board advertising spaces. Reserve your board today to be a part of one of the most effective advertising opportunities!

Ice Resurfacer
Crowds gather around as the famous Zamboni takes the ice. Make it your company's advertisement they see as we resurface the ice at least 8 times a day.

In Ice Logos
Place your company logo in the ice. Spectators all around the mall will think of you every time they look at the ice.

Other Opportunities
Include Bulletin Boards, Birthday Parties, Skating School, Holiday Ice Shows, Ice Crystal Classic Annual Competition and more!
