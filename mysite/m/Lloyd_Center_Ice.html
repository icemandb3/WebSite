Driving Directions

From I-5 North

From I-5 North
Take the Rose Quarter exit, stay in the left two lanes, and pass through the first light heading towards Weidler street. Turn left onto N Weidler St. Turn right at NE Martin Luther King Blvd. Turn left onto NE Multnomah St. Just before the Lloyd Center Cinema building turn left at the light into Lloyd Center Mall (you will be paralleling Sears and Marshalls). At the north end of Marshalls you will turn left followed by a quick right onto the ramp leading to the underground parking. When the ramp splits take the right (uphill) split. At the top of the ramp turn left and park in the small parking area. The bank of glass entrance doors are at the bottom of a ½ flight of stairs.

From I-84 West

I-84 West
Take the Lloyd Center exit. At the top of the exit ramp bear right onto NE 13th Ave. Turn right onto NE Multnomah St. Just before the Lloyd Center Cinema building turn left at the traffic light into Lloyd Center Mall (you will be paralleling Sears and Marshalls). At the north end of Marshalls you will turn left followed by a quick right onto the ramp leading to the underground parking. When the ramp splits take the right (uphill) split. At the top of the ramp turn left and park in the small parking area. The bank of glass entrance doors are at the bottom of a ½ flight of stairs.

Large van or bus parking directions

Large van or bus parking directions:
At the north end of Marshalls do not take the quick right turn into the underground parking lot. Instead, turn left at the corner of Marshalls. Continue straight across the parking lot to the sheltered stairway located at the west end of the parking area. Park near the shelter. To enter the mall, take the stairs down to the 1A parking level. Walk across the small parking area toward the bank of glass entrance doors are at the bottom of a ½ flight of stairs.
