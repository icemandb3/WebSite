Kool Kamp 2013

Brochure Kool Kamp 2013

Starts June 17 and runs weekly

Been Thinking of Kool Kamp?    Check out the fun!


Join us for the best camp in town! For ages 6 to 12:

Each day will be filled with activities! Some activities on the agenda are...skating, crafts, exercises, coloring, and puzzles. Ice time is 10-12 and 2-4 with lesson included. Bring a bag lunch or make arrangements to purchase one from our snack bar. Snacks provided. Camps run weekly 6/18-8/31, 8-5:30pm with early drop off and late pick up available 1/2 hour before and after camp.. T-shirt included the first week of camp. Ages 6 to 12, sorry, no one under age 6.

A $50 nonrefundable deposit holds your spot. Kamp costs $219 per week ($169 if you paid the deposit), 4 weeks costs $795. (No other discounts if you go for the four week deal.)
